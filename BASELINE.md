## Release timeline

The release date has moved twice. Trailer 1 (December 5, 2023) implied a Fall 2025 window. In May 2025, alongside Trailer 2, Rockstar gave the first firm date of **May 26, 2026**. That later slipped to the current **November 19, 2026**, which Rockstar framed as extra time for polish. Digital pre‑loading opens **November 12, 2026**, seven days ahead of launch.

## Platforms — and the PC question

GTA6 is confirmed for PS5, PS5 Pro, Xbox Series X and Xbox Series S. **There is no announced PC version** — no platform, no date. Historically Rockstar ships PC months after console, but nothing is official. This is the single highest‑value thing the monitor watches: the reveal will most likely surface first as a Steam / Epic / Rockstar Launcher product page going live, or as a `pc` / `steam` / `epic` string appearing in the site's JS bundle, before any Newswire post.

## Editions & pricing

Two editions at launch. **Standard ($79.99)** includes the base game and one month of GTA+, plus the Vintage Vice City pre‑order pack for those who pre‑order. **Ultimate ($99.99)** adds exclusive vehicles, weapons and apparel, plus exclusive missions and story content and exclusive in‑game shops (Rideout Customs and One‑Eyed Willie's mod shops, clothing stores, tattoo parlours) that are closed in Standard.

Ultimate's named contents: the '95 Grotti Cheetah and '55 Vapid Stanier vehicles; Hawk & Little Morgan Revolvers plus personalized variants of Jason's Girardi ES9 and Lucia's Klose K17 pistols; and Jason's vintage pastel linen suit and Lucia's red sequin mini dress. An open question is whether a Collector's or physical special edition exists beyond these two.

## World & story

The game is set in the **state of Leonida**, Rockstar's fictional Florida, centered on a modern **Vice City** (Miami). Six regions are officially named: Vice City, the Leonida Keys, Grassrivers, Mount Kalaga, Port Gellhorn, and Ambrosia. It's billed as the biggest GTA world yet (commonly estimated 1.5–2× GTA V, though Rockstar has put no official number on it). The story is a Bonnie‑and‑Clyde‑style crime saga following dual protagonists **Lucia Caminos** and **Jason Duval**.
